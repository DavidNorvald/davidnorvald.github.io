Hello! My name is Dave!

I have always been around computers and technology my whole life.
While starting out as a general interest and fascination, it has become a learning hobby, and is now turning into a new career path.

While knowing the rudimentary basics of computers while building my own, I never knew exactly what went on behind the scenes, other then what I had seen in those Hollywood movies of the computer hackers making it look too easy.
What really set me in motion of wanting to learn more about computers was constantly having to wait for the IT department to help resolve my issues while owning my own business.
After getting fed up with slow response times, I would end up researching the issue myself online to try and find a solution. Generally, I would have my issue resolved before my tech support team would get to my issue.
After having done this multiple times, I would troubleshoot the issue myself, only contacting the IT department if it was an issue I could not resolve myself, mostly because I did not have access to certain systems. Troubleshooting my own problems led me to learn more about computers and how they work in general and peaked my own curiosity.
